THE NETHER

This is the Basalt Delta

The Basalt Delta has a lot of useless blocks except for black stone and basalt

Black Stone is used for crafting useful tools if you are interested in starting a nether survival.

It is the same as stone in the overworld and you can craft Stone tools with it

Basalt on the other hand doesn't have really much real use but it is good for decoration when it comes to creating some nether style house

This is the Warped Forest

The Warped Forest is full of enderman and bluish wood you can mine

Warped logs are used mainly for supplies in case you run out of wood when you enter the nether

It is the same as wood in the overworld and you can craft a lot of things with it

Wapred fungus is used to grow more wapred trees and it can also be used to scare of a vile creature -- hoglin

This is the Crimson Forest

The Crimson Forest contains a lot of piglins whom you can trade gold ingot with

Crimson logs are used mainly for supplies in case you run out of wood when you enter the nether

It is the same as wood in the overworld and you can craft a lot of things with it

Crimson fungus is used to grow more wapred trees
