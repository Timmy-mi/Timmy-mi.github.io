How to play -- noob version -- surving the first night
  1. Break only one at least 10 trees
  2. Craft a crafting table using four planks
  3. Craft a wooden pickaxe
  4. Mine Stone and upgrade you pickaxe to a stone pickaxe
  5. Kill Some sheep
  6. Make a bed using 3 wool and 3 planks
  7. Click on the bed and skip the day
